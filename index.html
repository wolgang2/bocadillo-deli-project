Our Story

About Bocadillo

Bocadillo Deli Bodega was built on one simple belief: food made with real ingredients, real craft, and real care will always win. Tucked in the heart of Canton, Ohio, we are the neighborhood spot that refuses to cut corners — and our guests taste the difference every single visit.

Our menu draws from the best of American deli culture: Detroit-style pizza baked to a crackling caramelized edge, Chicago-style hot dogs loaded with the classic toppings that made them legendary, and gourmet hoagies & wraps stacked high with premium meats, house-made sauces, and the freshest produce we can source. Everything is made to order. Nothing sits under a heat lamp.

Bocadillo is more than a meal — it is a moment. Come in hungry, leave satisfied, and bring everyone you know. Canton deserves a place like this, and we are proud to be it.
